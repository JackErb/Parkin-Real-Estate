Guy Parkin

Real Estate Professional in Coeur D Alene, Idaho United States

Guy Parkin is new to the Northwest like so many of our clients and customers. Originally from Australia, he has travelled and worked globally. His love of people and food have him telling stories from all over the world. Now residing in Spokane and again on the move to Post Falls mid year 2018.

What brought him to this beautiful region other than the four seasons, great lakes, rivers, mountains, skiing, fishing , hunting and golf ??? You guessed it ….the love of his life which he met way back in 1979 as a Rotary exchange student in San Antonio , Texas.

Guy and Linda married here in Coeur d’Alene in July 2016. He fell in love again with the girl and the North West. “ I’m here to stay” Guy quips! “It doesn’t get much better”…. Coming from an Automotive retail management and training base which allowed Guy to travel so widely. A highly recognised prestige brand facilitator / trainer internationally in his own right. He understands what customers want.

It will be an honor to be of service to you. Let's explore this area together and find that investment or family home that is right for you.
